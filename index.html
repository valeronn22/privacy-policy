Privacy Policy – RoyalX

Last updated: 04/12/2025

1. Introduction

RoyalX ("the App") is a media player application designed to allow users to access and play content from their own compatible media servers or service providers.

The App does not provide any content of its own, does not host, store, distribute, or index any media, and does not offer subscriptions or streaming services. Users are solely responsible for providing their own content sources.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal data, including:

  • Name, email address, or personal identifiers
  • Device identifiers
  • Analytics or usage data
  • Location data
  • Payment information
  • Login credentials entered by the user

All credentials or URLs entered by the user are processed only locally on the device and are never transmitted to RoyalX or to any server owned or controlled by the App.

3. Information Processed Locally

The App may temporarily process the following information only on the device:

  • Server URL
  • Username and password for the user's own service
  • App preferences and configuration

4. No Third-Party Data Sharing

The App does not share any user information with third parties, advertising networks, analytics systems, or external companies.

5. Children's Privacy

RoyalX is suitable for general audiences and does not collect any data from children or adults.

6. Security

Since the App does not collect or store any personal information, there is no risk of unauthorized access to such data. Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of any credentials they choose to enter.

7. External Services

The App may allow users to enter credentials for third-party media services. These services have their own privacy policies. RoyalX is not responsible for their practices or content.

8. Changes to This Policy

RoyalX may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page.
